A guide to gender-related data resources on the countries of Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Mongolia, Tajikistan, Turkmenistan, and Uzbekistan

{{< /blocks/cover >}} {{% blocks/lead color="primary" %}} The Central Asia and Mongolia Gender Data Portal (CAMGDP) is a resource created by the Rutgers University - New Brunswick Libraries to assist scholars, academics, activists, and students in finding gender-related data on Central Asia and Mongolia. We compile quantitative and qualitative sources, informational websites, media publications, and various level organizations that work in Kazakhstan. Kyrgyzstan, Tajikistan, Turkmenistan, Uzbekistan, and Mongolia. Our focus on gendered data comes from personal research interests and observations of the lack of gender-related data portals on Central Asia and Mongolia. {{% /blocks/lead %}} {{< blocks/section color="white" >}} {{% blocks/feature icon="fa fa-book" title="Guide" url="/about/" %}} Read our guide to the resources on CAMGDP. {{% /blocks/feature %}} {{% blocks/feature icon="fa fa-globe" title="Resources" url="/blog/" %}} Browse or search the full listing of resources. {{% /blocks/feature %}} {{% blocks/feature icon="fab fa-github" title="Suggestions welcome!" url="https://github.com/ryandata/camgdp_docsy" %}} Source code and materials on **GitHub**.
