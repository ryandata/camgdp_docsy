Founded in 2018, [Kazakhstan International Bureau for Human Rights and Rule of Law (KIBHR)](https://bureau.kz/) is a Kazakhstani bureau of human rights. It engages in human rights advocacy, shares relevant reports, and engages in human rights monitoring in Kazakhstan.
